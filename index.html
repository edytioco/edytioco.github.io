About Me

My name is Ethan, and I graduated with a bachelor's in computer engineering at Stevens Institute of Technology in 2020. I started programming in C++ my sophomore year of high school, and then Java my junior and senior year.

Come Stevens, I was a teaching assistant for both freshman and graduate level C++ courses, and also tutored data structures. I've also taken a course for Pyton and Java each at Stevens, and on my own time I'm picking up HTML and CSS from CodeAcademy. So that's the STEM side of me :)



War of Rights: the 1st NJ Volunteer Infantry Regiment

Right around 2020, I became a huge history fan when I joined an organized group in a Civil War FPS game called War of Rights.

At the end of January, a high school friend invited me to join the 1st New Jersey Volunteer Infantry Regiment. I entered the group as a volunteer, went through training around February, and became a Private by the opening of March 2020.

Since then, I've joined them in Sunday night skirmishes, which has been an absolutely wonderful experience of historical drill, tactics, and camaraderie. In fact, I have a few videos up about them!
